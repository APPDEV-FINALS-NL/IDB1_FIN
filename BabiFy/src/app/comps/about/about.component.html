About us

BabiFy is an E-shop that provides the needs of your baby in a convenient way.

BabiFy was founded in 2023 by Dominic Laroya and Ericka Nasingao with a vision of building the best online store in the Cordillera for infant's clothing. Our goal is to inspire our customers by offering an exclusive shopping experience and excellent customer service with the best mixture of well-known and high-quality brands.

Our product assortment is comprised of baby clothes; babysuits, bibs, tshirts, pants, hats, mittens and much more for ages 0-2.

You can find BabyFy Online Shop, on your mobile or in our flagship store in HappyPlace 123.
